The fine mascots of our cause. Varying in tempermant, and coloring.


Penny

Penny is a three year old tortoiseshell who always looks like she's seen a ghost. No, we don't know why she looks like that. She's perfect the way she is. Penny is a charmer who loves to escape outside.


Pepper

Penny's mean younger sister. Pepper has recently turned one, and she's slowly revealing her big girl hobbies. These include watching TV, stealing her sister's food, and playing fetch.


Meatball

Meatball is a ten year old senior who loves licking plastic. He also has incredible strength that he uses for evil: opening doors and dresser drawers.


Rikki

Short for Richard. Rikki is an eight year old man that works overtime at the biscuit factory. Fun fact about Rikki: he needed a sex change due to a traumatizing night in a rainstorm.
